## Title
Login allows password shorter than 6 characters

## Steps to Reproduce
1. Open the login page  
2. Enter a valid email  
3. Enter a password shorter than 6 characters  
4. Click the "Login" button  

## Actual Result
The system allows login with a password shorter than 6 characters.

## Expected Result
The system should not allow login with a password shorter than 6 characters.

## Environment
OS: Windows 10  
Browser: Google Chrome
Device: Laptop

## Type
Functional  

## Severity
Major  

## Priority
High
