## Title
Cart total price does not update when multiple items are added

## Description
The total price in the cart does not update when multiple items are added. The system displays the price for only one item regardless of quantity.

## Steps to Reproduce
1. Open the website
2. Add 1 item to the cart
3. Open the cart page
4. Add one more item
5. Check the total price

## Actual Result
The total price remains the same and reflects only one item.

## Expected Result
The total price should update according to the number of items in the cart.

## Environment
- OS: Windows 10
- Browser: Chrome
- Device: Laptop

## Severity
Critical

## Priority
High

## Type
Functional
